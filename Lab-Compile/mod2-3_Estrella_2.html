One Piece: The Straw Hat Crew

In the hit anime series One Piece the pirate crew of protagonists reffered to as The Straw Hat Crew consists of Nico Robin, Nami, Brook, Sanji, Monkey D. Luffy (front), Jimbei (back), Tony Tony Chopper, Roronoa Zoro, Franky, and Usopp in their journey to the Grand Line to obtain Gold Roger's Treasure the One Piece, and for Luffy to become the next King of The Pirates."
